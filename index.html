Sales Call Companion & QA
01. CALL OPENING
Scripted Opening
"Thank you for calling [Hotel Name] reservations, my name is [Your Name]. Are you trying to make a reservation?"
02. CALLBACK NUMBER
Scripted Hook
"In case we get disconnected, may I have your best callback number?"
03. IHG REWARDS CHECK
Loyalty Spiel
"Are you an IHG One Rewards member? I can look up your account to get you points and exclusive rates!"
04. ASSUMPTIVE PROBING
Discovery Prompts
"What dates are you looking to stay?"

"Do you have any rooms or rates in mind?"
05. CREATE URGENCY
Urgency Frameworks (Choose One)
• "We're quite busy this month, let me see what I can squeeze in!"

• "We get a lot of last-minute calls due to cancellations—let me check what I have..."

• "Rates can change quickly, let's lock something in while it's available."
06. INITIAL OFFER
Pitch Options
"Great news! I can offer you our [room type] with (amenities) at [Best Flex Rate]. I can secure this..."

"Or for a little more, I can upgrade you to a 'suite [room type]' with (suite amenities) for [Rate] night plus tax—great value for more space and comfort!"
07 & 08. UPSELL & EXTRAS
Value Add-ons
• "Would you like to extend your stay while we still have availability?"

• "Do you need additional rooms for family, friends, or colleagues as well?"

• "For just $10 more, we can include breakfast in your stay—would you like to add that?"

Confirmation Formula
"So you're booked for [dates] in a [room type]. Let's make sure everything matches perfectly..."
